Season 3 starts the moment you load your profile after May 28th, 2020 12:00AM US Eastern Time! With this new season the following new leaderboards will be added: Assists, Betrayal, and Headshot season leaderboards.

Assists, Betrayal and Headshot leaderboards are now live and added to your profiles!

GAME HISTORY

Implemented GAME HISTORY to show for individual profiles! Players are now able to track their session overview that is currently separated by an hour. So if a player stops playing a game for an hour that concludes their session and a new one will be made in it's place! Match history displays the following: halo game, map, kills, deaths, assists, KD, KAD.
